About Me

I am an Associate Professor at the College of Life Science and Technology, Huazhong University of Science and Technology. My research interests include readout electronics for medical imaging devices, ultrasonic imaging, high-performance reconfigurable hardware, ultra-fast signal sampling and processing, mobile sensing, deep learning, signal processing, and embedded system design.

Research Goals

  • Earable sensing
  • Ultrasonic sensing for medical and industrial applications
  • Multi-channel high-speed data acquisition systems
